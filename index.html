Who Are We?

Welcome to ESS Academy, a private football development environment dedicated to guiding athletes from grassroots to the professional game. We cultivate a culture that marries technical mastery with mental resilience, ensuring players not only survive but excel under elite‑level stress. Founded by Zachary Scheufele, ESS brings Barça‑inspired methodology and MLS Next‑proven results to athletes committed to genuine transformation.

Our Philosophy

At ESS, intelligent repetition fuels mastery. We empower players with a toolbox of ball control, decisive 1v1 dominance, and tactical IQ—sharpened in sessions that fuse relentless practice with reflective learning. Players are coached to be brave, creative, and unshakable when the stakes are highest.
